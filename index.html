Human Perplexity Test

Here's the game: You can only see two characters of the passage at first. You need to guess the next character, by typing it. (Just type it!) Your choices are the letters of the English alphabet (case insensitive) together with the space, comma, and period. That's 29 possibilities for every character. The correct text appears here:
